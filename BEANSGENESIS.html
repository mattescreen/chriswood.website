BEANS GENESIS 01: date night

date night creates a physically and sonically shared space between two participants - creating a unique shared identity through each performance!

it functions by situating two performers across from each other, each holding two contact points, using their bodily resistance to control the pitch of their respective low-frequency oscillators. these oscillators are then summed in a way that allows each one to cross-drive the other, resulting in a constant co-modulation of the output signal
